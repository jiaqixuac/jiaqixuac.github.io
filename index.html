Jiaqi Xu

Researcher, Huawei Noah's Ark Lab

I am currently a researcher at Huawei Noah's Ark Lab, focusing on multimodal image/video generation, editing and low-level vision. We are currently seeking self-motivated research interns — feel free to contact me if you are interested.

I got my Ph.D. from The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) in 2025, advised by Prof. Pheng-Ann Heng and Prof. Qi Dou. I received my M.Sc. in Information Engineering from CUHK and B.Eng. in Biomedical Engineering from Beihang University.
